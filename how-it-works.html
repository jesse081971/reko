The 6 steps

Each step makes the next step easier. Together they form a predictable operating system for local food.

1. Discover

Customers explore producers, products, and seasonal availability in one place.

2. Pre-order

Customers place orders before pickup day so demand becomes real and measurable.

3. Aggregate

Orders consolidate by pickup location so markets and hubs operate with clarity.

4. Fulfill

Producers pack to confirmed orders. Customers pick up quickly with fewer errors.

5. Analyze

See what sells, who buys, and what drives repeat purchases and retention.

6. Adapt

Improve timing, assortment, and engagement. Grow reliable demand over time.
