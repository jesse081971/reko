FAQs

Do we need to change our whole market model?

No. Many markets start by adding pre-orders as an option. You can expand to additional pickups once it is consistent.

What if producers already use their own tools?

REKO is designed to simplify participation, not increase admin. Standard listings and ordering reduce back-and-forth.

How do we measure success?

Start with order volume, repeat buyers, producer participation, and pickup reliability. Then improve week over week.

Ready to make demand visible early?

Start with a single pickup point. Expand as you learn what your community wants.
