FAQs

Do I have to attend every market day?

No. Many producers deliver to a pickup window. The market or hub coordinates customer pickup.

Can I limit quantities?

Yes. You can list what you can reliably produce. Scarcity stays honest and predictable.

How do customers find me?

Customers browse the marketplace and discover producers through markets, hubs, and community groups.

Ready to fulfill to confirmed orders?

Join one pickup point first. Expand once your weekly loop is smooth.
