## Core value proposition
```markdown
REKO is infrastructure for modern local commerce. It transforms farmers markets from weather-dependent events into predictable, data-driven marketplaces powered by pre-orders, automation and actionable insights.

Local food wins when convenience, trust and selection converge in one system. REKO makes that convergence operational.
```

## Hero promise
```markdown
Turn your local food economy into a predictable, scalable system.
REKO replaces hope with data, guesswork with visibility and one-day foot traffic with week-long commerce.
```

## Proof points
```markdown
- **Stabilized Revenue:** Pre-orders shift demand earlier in the week and stabilize revenue.
- **Improved Habits:** Median order size and repeat behavior increase when convenience improves.
- **Demand Certainty:** Producers gain demand certainty and reduce waste.
- **Market Visibility:** Markets gain visibility into what sells, who sells it and where growth comes from.
- **Local Autonomy:** RekoWurks provides centralized coordination with local autonomy.
```

## Page narrative arc
```markdown
1. **The Problem:** Farmers markets rely on foot traffic, weather and hope. Local food competes with national supply chains built on logistics and data.
2. **The Shift:** Local commerce needs infrastructure, not enthusiasm. Digital coordination must support physical markets.
3. **The System:** Introduce REKO as the operating layer: Discover → Pre-order → Aggregate → Pick up → Analyze → Improve.
4. **Economic Outcomes:** Show how predictable revenue, larger carts and stronger retention emerge from system design.
5. **Ecosystem Value:** Explain how producers, consumers and markets benefit differently from the same infrastructure.
6. **Vision:** Local food accessible everywhere because it runs on systems, not chance.
```
